description: Forced documentation of failures to ai_lessons.md
---

Initiate the reflection protocol after a failure, circuit breaker trip, or significant error correction.

**Workflow:**

1. Analyze the context of the recent failure (e.g., why a test failed repeatedly, why a pattern was incorrect).
2. Open `project-management/ai_lessons.md`.
3. Document the failure in a structured format:
   - **Trigger**: What caused the failure?
   - **Root Cause**: Why did the AI approach fail?
   - **Correction**: What is the correct approach for this codebase?
4. Ensure the lesson is actionable so future AI sessions do not repeat the mistake.
